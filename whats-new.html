Segneri is getting smarter

A new Daily Quiz joins six features inspired by the best language learning apps — built right into your Italian trainer.

✨ New in Version 2.2.1
🎴
New
Daily Quiz
Ten flip cards, self-graded. See the Italian word, tap to reveal the English meaning and example sentence — the word is spoken aloud on every flip. Mark yourself right ✓ or wrong ✗, then get your score. Missed some? Run the same ten again. Perfect round? Grab ten fresh words and keep going.
👂
New
Listening Mode
Hear a word spoken in Italian and identify it from four choices — no text shown. Perfect for training your ear, not just your eyes. Access it inside any pack via the new headphones tab.
📝
New
Sentence Mode (Cloze)
Every word in Segneri comes with a real example sentence. Now you can practice by filling in the missing Italian word in context — the way language actually lives. Powered by the built-in example sentences across all 2,005 words.
🎤
New
Pronunciation Practice
See a word, then say it aloud. Your microphone listens and tells you whether your Italian pronunciation was recognized correctly. Uses the Web Speech API — works best in Chrome and Safari on iOS 17+.
📅
New
Daily Review Queue
Words you've struggled with are automatically queued for review based on your history. The home screen now shows how many words are due today — tap to jump straight into your personalized review session.
📊
New
Pack Mastery %
Every pack on the home screen now shows your mastery percentage — how many words you've seen and rated correctly at least twice. Watch the number climb as you study.
❌
New
Weak Words List
A dedicated view of the words you miss most — ranked by error count across all packs. Tap any word to open its detail card, or start a focused practice session on your weakest words only.
⚡ Already in the App
🃏
Core
Flashcard Mode
Classic flip-cards with Italian on one side, English on the other. Rate each word Easy, Good, or Hard to feed the spaced repetition engine. Keyboard shortcuts: Space to flip, arrow keys to rate.
⌨️
Core
Challenge Mode
Type the Italian word from the English prompt. Levenshtein-based typo tolerance means one small slip doesn't mark you wrong — but you still have to get it right. Great for active recall.
🧠
Core
Quiz Mode (4-Choice MCQ)
Multiple choice — see the Italian word, pick the right English meaning from four options. Smart distractors are pulled from the same pack so wrong answers feel plausible, not random.
⚡
Core
Spritzi Speed Game
Five choices, three lives, a live streak counter, and a ticking clock. The fastest, most intense way to drill vocab in Segneri. High scores trigger confetti. Lose all lives and it's game over.
🔁
Core
Spaced Repetition
Words you struggle with appear more often; words you've mastered fade to the back. Bucket-based weighting keeps every session efficient — always working on what you need most.
🔊
Core
Italian Text-to-Speech
Every word and example sentence can be read aloud using native Italian voices via the Web Speech API. Tap the speaker icon anywhere in the app, or enable auto-speak in flashcard mode.
📈
Core
XP, Streaks & Stats
Earn XP for every session, maintain a daily streak, and track your progress across all packs in the Progress tab. Your stats persist in localStorage — no account needed.
🌅
Core
Word of the Day
A fresh Italian word greets you on the home screen every morning, complete with pronunciation, IPA, and an example sentence. A quiet daily nudge to keep your Italian moving.
🔤
Core
IPA Pronunciation Guide
Every one of the 2,005 words includes its International Phonetic Alphabet transcription. Toggle IPA display on or off in any study mode — useful for learners who read phonetics.
🔡
Core
Conjugation Tables
Tap any of the 16 key verbs to see full present, past, and future conjugation tables — all six persons, formatted cleanly. A quick grammar reference built right into the trainer.
🌙
Core
Dark / Light Theme
Switch between dark and light modes with a tap. Your preference is saved. The app defaults to dark — easier on the eyes for late-night study sessions.
📱
Core
Works Like a Native App (PWA)
Add Segneri to your iPhone or Android home screen for a full-screen, no-browser-chrome experience. A service worker caches all 2,005 words locally so the app works offline — no internet needed after first load.
📦
Core
20 Themed Vocabulary Packs
Words organized into 20 real-life topics: Greetings, Restaurant, Travel, Verbs, Family, Body, Health, Shopping, Home, Nature, Animals, Work, Technology, Sports, Arts, Emotions, and more. Study a topic, or mix them all.
